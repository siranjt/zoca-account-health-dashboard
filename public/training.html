Module 9 · Case file

Solve a real at-risk case

Read the file, then click the right actions to move them into the Save Plan. Close the case to certify.
Bliss Nail Lounge
Miami, FL · AM You · 9 months with Zoca
  • Composite 23 — value is dragging (leads down 38% over 30 days).
  • Billing: $249 MRR, 12 days overdue.
  • Web app: Discovery Web on, Casual on Command Center (2 / 28 days).
  • 1 open support ticket — "booking link not working".
Your job
Build the save-play. Move the three right actions into the plan — leave the traps out.
Available actions — click to add0 / 3 correct
🔧 Resolve the overdue invoice
📍 Verify GBP & run a re-engagement push
🛒 Upsell a brand-new product
🎫 Fix the booking-link ticket
😴 Do nothing — just monitor
▼ Save Plan — click actions to add them here
The logic
Value is dragging and they're overdue — a high churn risk. The play: fix the money (overdue), fix the results (GBP + re-engagement), clear the blocker (ticket). Upselling a struggling account, or "just monitoring" a churn risk, are the traps. This is exactly the kind of case Alfred can draft for you.
